How are The Three Linked?

THE TRINITY :Imagine a Triangle. The three points are our topics, but the magic happens on the lines connecting them.

The Foundation: GEOMETRY
Geometry is not just shapes; it is the source code of the universe. Before a thing can exist, it must have a form. Geometry provides the rules (physics, dimensions, ratios)
  • The Link to Art : Art uses Geometry to create harmony (The Gol world ie; 3D printers, Laser Cutters, CNC Machines.
The Engine: Technology
We, as humans, have limited precision. We shake, we get tired. Technology (code, machines) Executes Geometric rules with perfect precision, millions of times per second.
  • The Link to Geometry: Tech makes complex Geometry possible. You cannot draw a fractal by hand (it takes too long), but a computer can iterate the Geometry instantly.
  • The Link to Craft : Tech moves digital ideas into the physical world 3D Printers, Laser Cutters, CNC Machines.
The Output: ART & CRAFT
Art is the human reaction to the order of Geometry. Concept: If it's just math, it's cold. If it's just Tech, it's robotic. "Craft" is where human intuition tweaks the parameters to make it feel something
  • The Link to Tech : "Generative Art" is when you tell the Tech to be random. You give the machine the brush, but you set the rules.
  • The Link to Geometry : Craft is the physical manifestation of Geometric laws . weaving, origami, carpentry, joints.
